Our Story

Baked with Heart,
Served with Joy

Since 1956, Monginis has been synonymous with celebration. What started as a single bakery in Mumbai has grown into India's most loved cake chain — but our core belief remains unchanged: every cake should feel like it was made just for you.

Our bakers use only the freshest ingredients — no shortcuts, no artificial flavours. Every morning, the ovens come alive before sunrise so your cakes are always at their very best.

  • 🌿 Fresh, natural ingredients sourced daily
  • 🎓 Trained master bakers with 10+ years of experience
  • 🚀 Same-day delivery across 500+ cities
  • 🎨 Fully customised cakes for every occasion
